InterledgerPacket org.interledger.codecs.CodecContext.read ( final InputStream  inputStream) throws IOException

Helper method that accepts an InputStream, detects the type of the packet to be read and decodes the packet to InterledgerPacket. Because InterledgerPacket is simply a marker interface, callers might prefer to utilize the functionality supplied by readAndHandle(InputStream, InterledgerPacket.Handler) or readAndHandle(InputStream, InterledgerPacket.VoidHandler).
